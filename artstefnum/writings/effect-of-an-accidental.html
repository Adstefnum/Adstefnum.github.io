Effect of an Accidental(Working on...)


What power has thou over me,
Ironic, even the greatest of men,
Yes, even the greatest agree that that sneaky fellow,
The tiny mistake,
A smudge in comparison to the greatness,
is responsible for their fall.
But never is he the ladder on which they rose.
The truth,
Sneaky, he is
But he is of constant ability,
For there are but two of him, yin and yang,
At times, they will work in tandem,
,
You have the ability of different forms
But you blame him for your failure
